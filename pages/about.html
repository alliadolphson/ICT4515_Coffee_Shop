Our Shop

We've been brewing home-roasted coffee from our expanded location at 1234 Street Address in Denver's Sunnyside Neighborhood since 2020.

Our Shop boasts comfortable seating both inside and out. Our patio is shaded for the summer and heated for the fall and winter months. And, yes, of course the WiFi reaches the patio outside! How else could your dog keep up to date with his followers on the Gram?

Stop in for a cup!

Ethically Sourced

Whether you prefer to buy organic because you are concerned about the effects of pesticides or want to be sure your morning brew is certified Fair Trade, we have you covered at Brewed Awakening.

All of our coffee is sourced from Nicaragua, Honduras, and El Salvador from small farms practicing sustainable farming methods. Our beans are certified by Fair Trade USA and we take pride in giving back to the communities who bring us the most delicision coffee you'll find anywhere in Denver.

Our Story

The idea for Brewed Awakening was born in 2015 with shop owner Calvin's first cup of freshly roasted coffee on a backpacking trip through El Salvador. Calvin brought this delicious, sustainable coffee home to Denver and opened a gathering place for residents of Sunnyside and beyond to come together and enjoy great coffee and conversation.

Whether you're looking for a great cup of coffee or a welcoming place to relax, work, or study, come stop in and see us. We can't wait to meet you!
